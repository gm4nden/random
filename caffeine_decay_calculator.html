bedtime
Red — Significant effects (above 75 mg): at this level caffeine meaningfully disrupts sleep architecture — lighter slow-wave sleep, reduced total sleep time, longer time to fall asleep.
Yellow — noticeable effects (25–75 mg): deep sleep still measurably reduced, sleep becomes more fragmented, and time to fall asleep is longer.
Green — minimal effects (below 25 mg): most people experience no measurable impact on sleep at this level.
